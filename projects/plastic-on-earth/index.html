Global Plastic Production

The chart below shows the increase of global plastic production from 1950 to 2019. From the graph, we can see that the plastic production increasing exponentially. Starting from only 2 million tonnes per year in 1950 to 460 million tonnes in 2019. The dent that is happening between 2009 and 2010 was mainly caused by 2008 global financial crisis.

Plastic Pollution per Country

Now, let's see who is the main contributor of the plastic pollution. The majority of the countries that contribute to plastic pollution are Asian countries and the majority of them are developing countries such as: Phillipines, Indonesia, Malaysia, India and many others.

How bad is it?

- Only a small amount of plastics are recycled and the rest of them end up in landfill/incinerated
- Plastic pollution reach Antartica, the most remote locations on Earth
- Marine animals get entangled in plastic waste
- Seabirds, turtles, whales, seaguls and many other animals ingested plastic waste
- Bring disease to marine animals and corals
